Interpretation Guide

Break Classification

  • Break Candidate: ΔMI dropped below median − 2×MAD threshold
  • Diagnostic Anomaly: Candidate that failed validation or below trigger threshold (vetoed ≠ blind)
  • Actionable Alert: Fully validated break with trigger enabled

    • Performance Metrics

  • Precision: Of actionable alerts, what fraction preceded a flare? (Higher = fewer false alarms)
  • Recall (structural): Of flares with structural precursors, what fraction were detected? (Low values expected)
  • F1 Score: Harmonic mean of Precision and Recall
  • Lead Time: Time between alert and flare onset (meaningful only for N≥3)

    • Important Notes

  • This is a structural precursor detector, not a flare detector
  • Low recall is expected: only flares with magnetically-mediated coupling signatures are in scope
  • Diagnostic anomalies remain interpretable even when alerts are vetoed
